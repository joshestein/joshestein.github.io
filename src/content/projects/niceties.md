The [Recurse Centre](https://recurse.com) has a really lovely initiative: at the
end of your batch, you can write 'niceties' to your fellow recursers. These are
kind parting words.

I loved this. It was a chance to reflect on my experiences, and to share my
feelings and gratitude for others.

I also like that I can go back at any time and view the niceties that I gave and
received. I can relive those experiences that were so meaningful to me, and see
what people valued in the way I held myself.

I wanted to create a similar thing for some researchers who have been spending
time together in Cape Town over the past few months. I wanted to be able to give
them some parting words. And... I have been wanting to learn Elixir/Phoenix for
some time! I just haven't had the motivation.

A winning combo! Something useful that will allow me to learn a new
language/framework.

You can find it: https://github.com/joshestein/niceties
